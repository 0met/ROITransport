About ROI Transport

Delivering excellence in logistics since 2020

Your Reliable Partner in Transportation & Logistics

ROI Transport is committed to providing outstanding customer service with dedicated trucks and/or trailer pools tailored for your specific needs. We offer storage, craning, rigging and drop trailer capacity for preloading for all our direct freight customers.

Our professional service has earned us recognition as a premiere heavy haul carrier in power gen industry. Our team specializes in managing projects with custom solutions specific to each customers needs. Our customers can count on us to handle every aspect of the project from start to finish with the highest level of care and precision.

All of our drivers have extensive experience in heavy haul and carry all necessary tarps, and equipment to protect and secure your freight. Our drivers keep in constant communication so you are updated about delivery appointments.

We operate throughout all 48 states, as well as Canada and Mexico. Let ROI Transport be your choice for your next project, we look forward to providing a turn key process that will logistically transform the way your freight is hauled.
